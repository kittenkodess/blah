About DOSE

We are a student-led initiative born from the passion and determination of Chicago high school students who refuse to stand by while their community struggles with drug misuse.

Our Story

Founded in Chicago's West Garfield area, DOSE emerged from the recognition that young voices can drive meaningful change in addressing substance abuse. Our team of dedicated high school students combines fresh perspectives with evidence-based approaches to create impactful solutions.

We believe that education, compassion, and community connection are the most powerful tools in combating drug misuse. Our pharmaceutical-inspired approach emphasizes precision, care, and scientific understanding in everything we do.
